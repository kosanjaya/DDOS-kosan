DoS

[ Service for Single Host or Botnets ]

Denial of Service is a cyber attack where the perpetrator tries to make a machine or network resource unavailable to the intended users by disrupting the service of the host connected to the Internet temporarily or indefinitely.

DDoS

[ Service for Master CNC ]

Distributed Denial of Service is a type of attack that is carried out by flooding internet network traffic on servers, systems, or networks. Generally this attack is carried out using several host computers of the attacker until the target computer cannot be accessed.
